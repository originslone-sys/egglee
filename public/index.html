FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is Egglee free to play? ▼
Yes! Every new player receives a free starter chicken upon connecting their wallet. You can start producing eggs and earning immediately without any initial investment.
How do I withdraw my earnings? ▼
Go to the Wallet section in your dashboard, enter the amount you'd like to withdraw, and confirm. USDT will be sent directly to your BSC wallet address. Withdrawals are processed promptly by our admin team.
What happens if my chickens run out of feed? ▼
Chickens that aren't fed enter starvation mode and stop producing eggs. If starvation continues beyond the limit (default 72 hours), they will die. Always keep your feed stock healthy!
Which blockchain does Egglee use? ▼
Egglee runs on Binance Smart Chain (BSC) using the BEP-20 USDT token. This means extremely low transaction fees and fast confirmations for all deposits and withdrawals.
Can I hatch my own eggs? ▼
Absolutely. Fertile eggs can be placed in the incubator. After the incubation period, they have a chance to hatch into a chick. Feed the chick until maturity and it becomes a fully productive chicken.
