🥚 Egg Production

Eggs are your primary source of income. Understanding how production works helps you maximize earnings.

Production Cycle

The game runs a production cycle every 10 minutes, totaling 144 cycles per day. During each cycle, every alive and fed chicken has a chance to produce an egg.

probability_per_cycle = eggs_per_day / 144

For example, a Common chicken (1.0 egg/day) has a 0.69% chance per cycle to produce an egg. Over 144 cycles, this averages out to ~1 egg per day. Production is probabilistic, so daily output may vary slightly.

🌾 Feed System

Feed is the fuel that keeps your farm running. Without it, your chickens stop producing and eventually die.

How Feed Works

Every production cycle (every 10 minutes), the system calculates the total feed needed by all your alive chickens:

feed_per_cycle = SUM(each_chicken.feed_per_day) / 144

If you have enough feed, it's automatically deducted and your chickens produce normally. If not, all your chickens enter starvation mode.

Incubation lets you turn eggs into new chickens — the only way to grow your flock without buying directly.

The Incubation Process

🥚 Available Egg → 🌾 Pay 0.7 Feed → ⏳ Incubate (72h) → 🎲 Hatch Roll (30%) → 🐣 Chick!

Expected Value Analysis

The expected value of incubating one egg:

EV = 0.30 × (0.70 × $1.20 + 0.25 × $3.00 + 0.05 × $7.00) - $0.084 - $0.10
EV = 0.30 × $1.94 - $0.184 = $0.582 - $0.184 = +$0.398

On average, each incubation attempt has a positive expected value of ~$0.40, making it a worthwhile strategy long-term, despite the 70% failure rate per attempt.

🐣 Chick Growth

When an egg hatches successfully, a chick is born. Chicks must be fed and raised before they become productive adult chickens.

Growth Requirements

A chick becomes an adult chicken when both conditions are met:

ConditionRequirement
Time elapsed since hatch12 days minimum
Total feed consumed2.0 units minimum

Feeding Chicks

Unlike adult chickens (which consume feed automatically), chicks must be manually fed by the player:

  • Go to the Farm tab and find the "Growing Chicks" section
  • Choose how much feed to give (between 0.1 and 5.0 units per feeding)
  • Feed is deducted from your feed balance and added to the chick's feed_consumed counter
  • You cannot over-feed beyond the required 2.0 units
🐣 Chick hatches → 🌾 Feed 2.0 units manually → ⏳ Wait 12 days → 🐔 Adult chicken!

Growth Cost

total_cost = 2.0 feed units × $0.12 = $0.24
Tip: Feed your chicks early! The 12-day timer starts at hatch, but the feed requirement must also be met. If you delay feeding, the chick will just sit there even after 12 days.

💀 Death Mechanics

Chickens can die in two ways. Both are permanent — dead chickens cannot be revived.

Natural Death (Lifespan)

Every chicken has a fixed lifespan determined by its species. When the lifespan expires, the chicken dies automatically at the next production cycle.

SpeciesLifespanTotal Eggs (theoretical)Total Revenue
Common183 days~183 eggs~$18.30
Premium210 days~483 eggs~$48.30
Rare270 days~1,485 eggs~$148.50

Starvation Death

If your feed balance runs out:

  1. All your chickens immediately stop producing eggs
  2. A starvation timer starts for each unfed chicken
  3. If you refill feed before the deadline, starvation is cleared and production resumes
  4. If a chicken goes 72 hours (3 days) without feed, it dies permanently
Critical: Starvation affects ALL your chickens at once. If you run out of feed with 20 chickens, all 20 start starving simultaneously. Refilling feed within 72 hours saves them all — failing to do so kills them all.
🌾 Feed runs out → ⚠️ Starvation starts → ⏰ 72h countdown → 💀 Death (permanent)

Your wallet holds two balances: USDT (from selling eggs) and Feed (for your chickens).

Earning USDT

You earn USDT by:

  • Selling eggs to the system: $0.10 per egg (instant)
  • Selling on the marketplace: Set your own price for eggs or chickens (P2P)
  • Depositing USDT: Send USDT (BEP-20) directly to the platform wallet

How Withdrawals Work

💡 Example

You request a withdrawal of $10.00. The 3% fee is $0.30, so you receive $9.70 USDT in your MetaMask wallet on BSC.

Deposits

To deposit USDT into Egglee:

  1. Copy the platform's deposit wallet address from the Wallet tab
  2. Send USDT (BEP-20 only) from your MetaMask
  3. Wait for 12 block confirmations (~36 seconds on BSC)
  4. Your balance is automatically credited
Important: Only send USDT on BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20). Tokens sent on other networks (Ethereum, Polygon, etc.) or wrong token contracts cannot be recovered.
