1. Overview

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how Egglee ("Company", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, protects, and shares information from users ("User", "you", "your") of the Egglee platform ("Platform"). By using the Platform, you consent to the practices described in this Policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency. This Policy applies to all interactions with the Platform, including website visits, wallet connections, gameplay, and transactions.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Wallet Address: Your public blockchain wallet address (e.g., MetaMask), used as your unique identifier on the Platform. This is your primary authentication credential.
  • Transaction Data: Details of purchases, sales, deposits, and withdrawals you initiate on the Platform, including amounts, timestamps, and transaction hashes.
  • Cryptographic Signatures: Signed messages used to verify wallet ownership during authentication. We do not store the signatures themselves after verification.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Browser type and version, operating system, screen resolution, and device type (desktop/mobile).
  • Connection Information: IP address, approximate geographic location (country/region level), internet service provider.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, session duration, click patterns, and interaction timestamps.
  • Blockchain Data: Public blockchain transaction data associated with your wallet address, including transaction history, token balances, and smart contract interactions on the BNB Smart Chain.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Private keys, seed phrases, or wallet passwords;
  • Real names, email addresses, or phone numbers (unless voluntarily provided for support);
  • Government-issued identification documents (unless required for enhanced compliance);
  • Financial information beyond blockchain transaction data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Platform Operation: To authenticate your identity, manage your account, process transactions, and deliver the core gameplay experience;
  • Security & Fraud Prevention: To detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent activity, unauthorized access, abuse of the Platform, multi-accounting, and other prohibited activities;
  • Economy Management: To monitor and maintain the health of the in-game economy, detect anomalies, and ensure fair gameplay;
  • Performance & Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, diagnose technical issues, optimize performance, and improve the Platform experience;
  • Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, and governmental requests, including anti-money laundering (AML) and counter-terrorism financing (CTF) obligations;
  • Communication: To provide important notices regarding Platform changes, security alerts, or Terms updates (delivered through the Platform interface).

4. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party providers who assist in Platform operations (hosting, infrastructure, analytics), bound by contractual confidentiality obligations;
  • Blockchain Network: Transactions are recorded on the public BNB Smart Chain blockchain. Wallet addresses and transaction data are inherently public and visible to anyone on the blockchain;
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, legal process, subpoena, court order, or governmental authority request;
  • Protection of Rights: When necessary to protect the rights, property, safety, or security of the Company, its users, or the public;
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, acquisition, reorganization, sale of assets, or bankruptcy, user information may be transferred as part of the transaction;
  • With Your Consent: In any other circumstance where you have provided explicit consent.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL protocols;
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access controls limit who can access user data within our team;
  • JWT Authentication: Session management uses JSON Web Tokens with expiration and cryptographic signing;
  • Rate Limiting: API endpoints are protected against brute-force attacks and abuse;
  • Secure Headers: HTTP security headers (Helmet.js) protect against common web vulnerabilities;
  • Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for suspicious activity, unauthorized access, and security anomalies.

Despite our security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data. You acknowledge that you provide information at your own risk.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Maintain your active account and provide the Services;
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, and accounting obligations;
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements;
  • Maintain security and prevent fraud.

After account termination, we may retain certain data (wallet address, transaction history) for a period required by applicable law or legitimate business purposes, after which it will be securely deleted or anonymized.

Blockchain data is permanent and immutable. Transaction records on the BNB Smart Chain cannot be deleted, modified, or removed by us or any party.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you;
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data;
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements and blockchain immutability;
  • Portability: Request a machine-readable copy of your data;
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities;
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing under certain circumstances;
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent for processing where consent is the legal basis, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through the official channels on the Platform. We will respond to requests within 30 days or as required by applicable law. We may request identity verification before fulfilling your request.

Note: Certain data (such as public blockchain transaction records) cannot be modified or deleted due to the inherent nature of blockchain technology.

8. Cookies & Local Storage

The Platform uses browser local storage (not traditional cookies) to:

  • Store authentication tokens (JWT) for session management;
  • Store user preferences and interface settings;
  • Maintain wallet connection state.

This data is stored locally in your browser and is not transmitted to third-party tracking services. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings, which will require you to re-authenticate.

9. Third-Party Services

The Platform interacts with the following third-party services:

  • MetaMask / Web3 Wallets: For authentication and transaction signing. Wallet providers have their own privacy policies;
  • BNB Smart Chain (BSC): Public blockchain network for processing USDT transactions;
  • Google Fonts: For loading the Inter font family. Subject to Google's privacy policy.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in jurisdictions other than your country of residence. By using the Platform, you consent to the transfer of your information to jurisdictions that may have different data protection standards. We implement appropriate safeguards to protect your data during international transfers.

11. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that a user is under 18, we will take steps to terminate their account and delete associated data.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the Platform interface. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the Policy was last revised. Continued use of the Platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the official support channels available on the Platform.
