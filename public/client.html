How Egglee Works

🏡

1. Connect Your Wallet

Click "Connect Wallet" (top right) to link your MetaMask wallet on the BNB Smart Chain network. This is your account — no email or password needed.

New users automatically receive a free Common chicken and 5 units of feed to get started!
🐔

2. Your Chickens Produce Eggs

Each chicken produces eggs automatically every day as long as it has feed. Different breeds produce at different rates:

  • Common — 1 egg/day, costs 1.00 USDT
  • Premium — 3 eggs/day, costs 10.00 USDT
  • Rare — 5 eggs/day, costs 20.00 USDT
Without feed, chickens starve and stop producing. Keep your feed stock up!
🥚

3. Sell Eggs for USDT

Click "Sell Eggs" on the dashboard to instantly sell all collected eggs at $0.12 per egg. The USDT is added to your in-game balance immediately.

Collect and sell daily for maximum earnings. Eggs accumulate but don't expire.
🌾

4. Buy Feed & Expand

Purchase feed with USDT via MetaMask to keep your chickens producing. You can also buy more chickens to increase your daily egg output.

  • Buy Feed — 10 units per purchase
  • Buy Chicken — choose the breed from the dropdown
  • Buy Eggs — buy eggs ($0.20) to incubate for new chicks
🐣

5. Incubate Eggs into Chicks

Go to the Farm tab to incubate eggs. Each incubation has a 30% hatch rate. Successful hatches give you a new chick that grows into a chicken — the breed is random based on rarity weights.

Incubation is a gamble — but hatched chickens are free, saving you USDT!
💰

6. Withdraw Your Earnings

Go to the Wallet tab to request a withdrawal. Your USDT balance is sent to your MetaMask wallet on BNB Smart Chain.

  • Withdrawals are reviewed manually (up to 72h)
  • A small fee is deducted from each withdrawal
  • Limit: 1 withdrawal per day
📈

7. Grow Your Farm

The key to maximizing earnings:

  • Reinvest — use egg profits to buy more chickens
  • Upgrade breeds — Premium and Rare earn more per day
  • Keep feed stocked — starving chickens produce nothing
  • Incubate often — free chickens from hatched eggs
Start with Common chickens, reinvest into better breeds as your balance grows.
